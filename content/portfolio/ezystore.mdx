## Overview

EzyStore (ezystore.ir) is a specialized online marketplace for designer and niche perfumes targeting the Iranian market. The platform offers authentic fragrances from top global brands — Tom Ford, Yves Saint Laurent, Lattafa, and more — with a strong Instagram-driven discovery and sales channel.

## Problem

The client had a growing, loyal Instagram following (@ezystore.ir) but no structured online storefront. Sales were scattered across DMs and manual coordination, making inventory management, order tracking, and payment processing unreliable and hard to scale.

## Solution

Designed and built a complete e-commerce solution on **WordPress + WooCommerce**:

- **Product catalog** organized by gender and season (warm/cold) with detailed fragrance descriptions
- **Pocket perfume line** — a unique product category for affordable, travel-friendly versions
- **Instagram integration** — product tags and social proof channels feed directly into the storefront
- **User accounts** with wishlist, order history, and tracking
- **Blog** for fragrance guides and brand spotlights to drive organic SEO traffic

## Results

- Launched a fully operational marketplace with hundreds of SKUs
- Eliminated order-tracking chaos by moving all sales to a structured checkout flow
- Active on multiple social platforms with integrated product discovery
- Established as a trusted reference for authentic fragrances online: *"مرجع آنلاین خرید انواع عطر"*

## Tech Stack

WordPress · WooCommerce · Persian-localized storefront · Instagram Shopping · Custom theme
